About US

Know your roots first, then the world. If you want to know this root, you have to go close to thethousand years of history, heritage, culture and unique natural beauty of Bengal scattered in 64districts of the country. Acquiring knowledge, educational tours, pleasure trips, vacations no matter what you say, the first thing you need to do to take your friends and family to a new place is to have a lot of information about that place and a proper travel plan.This is not a special place of interest in any one district, plan a few days together to visit all the places of interest in one or a few districts together, so that both leisure and pleasure match. Repeatedly going to the same place takes more time and cost.This time, I am trying to get all the different information about my 64 friends traveling in Bangladesh collected from different web sites and my own travel experiences in all the district travel missions across the country. Explore Bangladesh by following whichyou can plan your own and go out looking for roots with your family all places.To encourage and securefamily travel, these website are packed with everything from pictures, sightseeing, on-the-go medicaland safety services, which will help you make the most of your precious time.Our heritage, culture andenvironment are currently losing ground with increasing industrialization, the impact of urban life andthe spread of sky culture; The development of the tourism industry with the participation of all, thefuture generations will find its roots in the protection, reform and preservation of this invaluableesource.
